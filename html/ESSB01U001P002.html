Unit 1   Exercise 2
Write am, is or are.
1   The weather  is  nice today.
2   I    not rich.
I’m / am  not rich.
3   This bag    heavy.
This bag is heavy.
4   These bags    heavy.
These bags are heavy.
5   Look! There    Helen.
Look!  There’s / is Helen.
6   My brother and I    good tennis players.
My brother and I are good tennis players.
7   Emily    at home.   Her children    at school.
Emily is at home.   Her children are at school.
8   I    a taxi driver.   My sister    a nurse.
I’m / am a taxi driver.   My sister is a nurse.

Write true sentences, positive or negative.  Use is/isn’t or are/aren’t.

3   (my hands / cold) My
