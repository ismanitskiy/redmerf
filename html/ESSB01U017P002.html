My parents aren’t at home at the moment.  They’ve gone out.
4   There’s a new restaurant in town.  Have you    to it?
There’s a new restaurant in town.  Have you been to it?
5   Rebecca loves Paris.  She’s    there many times.
Rebecca loves Paris.  She’s been there many times.
6   Helen was here earlier, but I think she’s    now.
Helen was here earlier, but I think she’s gone now.
7   Where’s Jessica?’     ‘She’s not in the office.  I think she’s    home.’
