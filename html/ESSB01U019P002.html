Unit 19   Exercise 1
Write for or since.
1    Helen has been in Ireland  since  Monday.
2    Helen has been in Ireland  for  three days.

My aunt has lived in Australia for 15 years.
4    Tina is in her office.  She has been there    7 o’clock.
Tina is in her office.  She has been there since 7 o’clock.
5    India has been an independent country    1947.
India has been an independent country since 1947.
6    The bus is late.  We’ve been waiting    20 minutes.
The bus is late.  We’ve been waiting for 20 minutes.
7    Nobody lives in those houses.  They have been empty    many years.
Nobody lives in those houses.  They have been empty for many years.

October.
Michael has been ill for a long time.  He has been in hospital since October.
