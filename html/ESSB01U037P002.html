Unit 37   Exercise 3
Write there is / there isn’t / is there or there are / there aren’t / are there.
1   Kentham isn’t an old town.  There aren’t  any old buildings.
2   Look!    a picture of your brother in the newspaper!
Look!  There’s / There is  a picture of your brother in the newspaper!
3   ‘Excuse me,    a bank near here?’   ‘Yes, at the end of the street.’
‘Excuse me, is there a bank near here?’   ‘Yes, at the end of the street.’
4     five people in my family: my parents, my two sisters and me.
There are five people in my family: my parents, my two sisters and me.
5   ‘How many students    in the class?’   ‘Twenty.’
‘How many students are there in the class?’   ‘Twenty.’
6   The road is usually very quiet.    much traffic.
The road is usually very quiet.  There isn’t much traffic.
7   ‘    a bus from the city centre to the airport?’   ‘Yes, every
20 minutes.’
‘Is there a bus from the city centre to the airport?’   ‘Yes, every 20 minutes.’
8   ‘    any problems?’   ‘No, everything is OK.’
‘Are there any problems?’   ‘No, everything is OK.’
9     nowhere to sit down.   any chairs.
There’s / There is  nowhere to sit down.  There aren’t any chairs.

Unit 37   Exercise 5
Write there’s / is there or it’s / is it.
1   ‘ There’s  a train at 10.30.’    ‘ Is it  a fast train?’
2   I’m not going to buy this shirt.    too expensive.
I’m not going to buy this shirt.   It’s too expensive.
3   ‘What’s wrong?’    ‘  something in my eye.’
‘What’s wrong?’    ‘There’s something in my eye.’
4     a red car outside your house.    yours?
There’s a red car outside your house.  Is it yours?
5   ‘  anything good on TV tonight?’    ‘Yes,    a programme
I want to see at 8.15.’
‘Is there anything good on TV tonight?’    ‘Yes, there’s a programme
I want to see at 8.15.’
6   ‘What’s that building?’    ‘    a school.’
‘What’s that building?’    ‘It’s a school.’
