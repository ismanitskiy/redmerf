C
Questions beginning Is … ? / Do … ? / Can … ? etc. (yes/no questions)
Compare:
Is Jack at home?
Have they got a car?
Can Ben swim?
Do they live near here?
Did anybody see you?
but
Do you know
I don’t know
if
or
whether
Jack is at home
they’ve got a car
Ben can swim
they live near here

?

You can use if or whether in these sentences:
Do you know if they’ve got a car?   or
Do you know whether they’ve got a car?
I don’t know if anybody saw me.   or
I don’t know whether anybody saw me.
