Unit 54   Exercise 4
Write to or for.
1    I went out to get some bread.
2    We went to a restaurant    have dinner.
We went to a restaurant to have dinner.
3    Robert wants to go to university    study economics.
Robert wants to go to university to study economics.
4    I’m going to London    an interview next week.
I’m going to London for an interview next week.
5    I’m going to London    visit some friends of mine.
I’m going to London to visit some friends of mine.
6    Do you have time    a cup of coffee?
Do you have time for a cup of coffee?
7    I got up late this morning. I didn’t have time    wash.
I got up late this morning. I didn’t have time to wash.
8    Everybody needs money    live.
Everybody needs money to live.
9    We didn’t have any money    a taxi, so we walked home.
We didn’t have any money for a taxi, so we walked home.
10    The office is very small. There’s space only    a desk and chair.
The office is very small. There’s space only for a desk and chair.
11    A: Excuse me, are you waiting   be served?
A: Excuse me, are you waiting to be served?
B: No, I’m already being served, thanks.
Unit 54   Exercise 5
Complete these sentences.  Choose from:
it / to arrive      you / tell me        them / change        the film / begin
1    We stopped at the lights and waited  for them to change .
2    I sat down in the cinema and waited   .
I sat down in the cinema and waited for the film to begin.
3    We called an ambulance and waited   .
We called an ambulance and waited for it to arrive.
4    ‘Do you know what to do?’   ‘No, I’m waiting   .’
