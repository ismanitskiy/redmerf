Unit 57   Exercise 1
Write make/making/made or do/doing/did/done.
1    ‘Shall I open the window?’    ‘No, it’s OK.  I’ll  do  it.’
2    What did you    at the weekend?  Did you go away?
What did you do at the weekend?  Did you go away?
3    Do you know how to    bread?
Do you know how to make bread?
4    Paper is    from wood.
Paper is made from wood.
5    Richard didn’t help me.  He sat in an armchair and    nothing.
Richard didn’t help me.  He sat in an armchair and did nothing.
6    ‘What do you    ?’    ‘I’m a doctor.’
‘What do you do?’    ‘I’m a doctor.’
7    I asked you to clean the bathroom.  Have you    it?
I asked you to clean the bathroom.  Have you done it?
8    ‘What do they    in that factory?’    ‘Shoes.’
‘What do they make in that factory?’   ‘Shoes.’
9    I’m    some coffee.  Would you like some?
I’m making some coffee.  Would you like some?
10    Why are you angry with me?  I didn’t    anything wrong.
Why are you angry with me?  I didn’t do anything wrong.
11    ‘What are you    tomorrow afternoon?’    ‘I’m working.’
‘What are you doing tomorrow afternoon?’    ‘I’m working.’

Unit 57   Exercise 3
Write make or do in the correct form.
1    I hate  doing  housework, especially cleaning.
2    Why do you always    the same mistake?
Why do you always make the same mistake?
3    ‘Can you   me a favour?’    ‘It depends what it is.’
‘Can you do me a favour?’    ‘It depends what it is.’
4    ‘Have you    your homework?’    ‘Not yet.’
‘Have you done your homework?’    ‘Not yet.’
5    I need to see the dentist, but I haven’t    an appointment.
I need to see the dentist, but I haven’t made an appointment.
6    I’m    a course in photography at the moment.  It’s very good.
I’m doing a course in photography at the moment.  It’s very good.
7    The last time I    an exam was ten years ago.
The last time I did an exam was ten years ago.
8    How many phone calls did you    yesterday?
How many phone calls did you make yesterday?
9    When you’ve finished Exercise 1, you can    Exercise 2.
When you’ve finished Exercise 1, you can do Exercise 2.
10    There’s something wrong with the car.  The engine is    a strange noise.
There’s something wrong with the car.  The engine is making a strange noise.
11    It was a bad mistake.  It was the worst mistake I’ve ever    .
It was a bad mistake.  It was the worst mistake I’ve ever made.
12    Let’s    a list of all the things we have to    today.
Let’s make a list of all the things we have to do today.
