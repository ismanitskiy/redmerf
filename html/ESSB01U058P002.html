Unit 58   Exercise 1
Write the correct form of have or have got.
1    I didn’t have  time to do the shopping yesterday.
(I / not / have)
2    ‘  Has Lisa got  or  Does Lisa have  a car?’     ‘No, she can’t drive.’
(Lisa / have?)
3    He can’t open the door.    a key.
(he / not / have)
He can’t open the door.  He doesn’t have / He hasn’t got  a key.
4      a cold last week.  He’s better now.
(Gary / have)
Gary had a cold last week.  He’s better now.
5    What’s wrong?    a headache?
(you / have?)
What’s wrong?  Do you have / Have you got  a headache?
6    We wanted to go by taxi, but    enough money.
(we / not / have)
We wanted to go by taxi, but we didn’t have enough money.
7    Laura is very busy.    much free time.
(she / not / have)
Laura is very busy.  She doesn’t have / She hasn’t got  much free time.
8      any problems when you were on holiday?
(you / have?)
Did you have any problems when you were on holiday?

Unit 58   Exercise 3
What do you say in these situations?  Use have.
1    Emily is going on holiday.  What do you say to her before she goes?
Have a nice holiday!
2    You meet Clare at the airport.  She has just got off her plane.  Ask her about the flight.
Did you have a good flight?
3    Tom is going on a long trip.  What do you say to him before he leaves?
Have a  nice / good  trip!
4    It’s Monday morning.  You are at work.  Ask Paula about her weekend.
Did you have a  nice / good  weekend?
5    Paul has just come home after playing tennis with a friend.  Ask him about the game.
Did you have a  nice / good  game (of tennis)?
6    Rachel is going out this evening.  What do you say to her before she goes?
Have a  nice / good  time!  or  Have a  nice / good  evening!  or  Have fun!
7    Mark has just returned from holiday.  Ask him about his holiday.
Did you have a  nice / good  holiday?
Unit 58   Exercise 4
Complete the sentences.  Use have/had and choose from the list.
an accident       a glass of water      a look
a walk                  a party                    something to eat
1    We  had a party  a few weeks ago.  We invited 50 people.
2    ‘Shall we    ?’    ‘No, I’m not hungry.’
‘Shall we have something to eat?’    ‘No, I’m not hungry.’
3    I was thirsty, so I    .
I was thirsty, so I had a glass of water.
4    I like to get up early and    before breakfast.
I like to get up early and have a walk before breakfast.
5    Tina is a very good driver.  She has never    .
Tina is a very good driver.  She has never had an accident.
6     There’s something wrong with the engine of my car.
       Can you    at it?
There’s something wrong with the engine of my car.
Can you have a look at it?
