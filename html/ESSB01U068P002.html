Unit 68   Exercise 3
Write a/an or some.
1    I read  a  book and listened to  some  music.
2    I need   money.  I want to buy   food.
I need some money.  I want to buy some food.
3    We met   interesting people at the party.
We met some interesting people at the party.
4    I’m going to open   window to get   fresh air.
I’m going to open a window to get some fresh air.
5    Rachel didn’t eat much for lunch – only   apple and   bread.
Rachel didn’t eat much for lunch – only an apple and some bread.
6    We live in   big house.  There’s   nice garden with 
beautiful trees.
We live in a big house.  There’s a nice garden with some beautiful trees.
7    I’m going to make a table.  First I need   wood.
I’m going to make a table.  First I need some wood.
8    Listen to me carefully.  I’m going to give you   advice.
Listen to me carefully.  I’m going to give you some advice.
9    I want to make a list of things to do.  I need   paper and   pen.
I want to make a list of things to do.  I need some paper and a pen.
