Unit 87   Exercise 4
Complete the sentences.  Use a comparative.
  1   Helen’s car isn’t very big.  She wants a  bigger  one.
  2   My job isn’t very interesting.  I want to do something  more interesting  .
  3   You’re not very tall.  Your brother is    .
You’re not very tall.  Your brother is taller.
  4   David doesn’t work very hard.  I work    .
David doesn’t work very hard.  I work harder.
  5   My chair isn’t very comfortable.  Yours is    .
My chair isn’t very comfortable.  Yours is more comfortable.
  6   Your idea isn’t very good.  My idea is    .
Your idea isn’t very good.  My idea is better.
  7   These flowers aren’t very nice.  The blue ones are    .
These flowers aren’t very nice.  The blue ones are nicer.
  8   My bag isn’t very heavy.  Your bag is    .
My bag isn’t very heavy.  Your bag is heavier.
  9   I’m not very interested in art.  I’m    in history.
I’m not very interested in art.  I’m more interested in history.
10   It isn’t very warm today.  It was    yesterday.
It isn’t very warm today.  It was warmer yesterday.
11   These tomatoes don’t taste very good.  The other ones tasted    .
These tomatoes don’t taste very good.  The other ones tasted better.
12   Britain isn’t very big.  France is    .
Britain isn’t very big.  France is bigger.
13   London isn’t very beautiful.  Paris is    .
London isn’t very beautiful.  Paris is more beautiful.
14   This knife isn’t very sharp.  Have you got a    one?
This knife isn’t very sharp.  Have you got a sharper one?
15   People today aren’t very polite.  In the past they were    .
People today aren’t very polite.  In the past they were more polite.
16   The weather isn’t too bad today.  Often it is much    .
The weather isn’t too bad today.  Often it is much worse.
