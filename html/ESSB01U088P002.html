Unit 88   Exercise 2
Complete the sentences.  Use than.
1   He isn’t very tall.  You’re  taller than him  or  taller than he is  .
2   She isn’t very old.  You’re    .
You’re older than her. / … than she is.
3   I don’t work very hard.  You work    .
You work harder than me. / … than I do.
4   He doesn’t watch TV very much.  You    .
You watch TV more than him. / … than he does.
5   I’m not a very good cook.  You    .
You’re a better cook than me. / … than I am.  or
You cook better than me. / … than I do.
6   We don’t know many people.  You    .
You know more people than us. / … than we do.
7   They don’t have much money.  You    .
You have more money than them. / … than they have.
8   I can’t run very fast.  You can    .
You can run faster than me. / … than I can.
9   She hasn’t been here very long.  You    .
You’ve been here longer than her. / … than she has.
10   They didn’t get up very early.  You    .
You got up earlier than them. / … than they did.
11   He wasn’t very surprised.  You    .
You were more surprised than him. / … than he was.
Unit 88   Exercise 3
Complete the sentences with a bit or much + comparative (older/better etc.).
1   Emma is 25.  Joe is 24.
Emma  is a bit older than Joe  .
2   Jack’s mother is 52.  His father is 69.
Jack’s mother    .
Jack’s mother is much younger than his father.
3   My camera cost £120.  Yours cost £112.
My camera    .
My camera cost a bit more than yours. / … than your camera.  or
My camera was a bit more expensive than yours / … than your camera.
4   Yesterday I felt terrible.  Today I feel OK.
I feel    .
I feel much better today than yesterday. / … than I did yesterday. /
… than I felt yesterday.
5   Today the temperature is 12 degrees.  Yesterday it was 10 degrees.
It’s    .
It’s a bit warmer today than yesterday. / … than it was yesterday.
6   Sarah is an excellent tennis player.  I’m not a very good player.
Sarah    .
Sarah is a much better tennis player than me. / … than I am.  or
Sarah is much better at tennis than me. / … than I am.  or
Sarah plays tennis much better than me. / … than I do.
