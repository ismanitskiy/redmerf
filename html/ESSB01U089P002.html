Unit 89   Exercise 2
Write sentences with as … as … .
1   Athens is older than Rome.
Rome  isn’t as old as Athens  .
2   My room is bigger than yours.
Your room isn’t    .
Your room isn’t as big as mine. / … as my room.
3   You got up earlier than me.
I didn’t    .
I didn’t get up as early as you. / … as you did.
4   We played better than them.
They    .
They didn’t play as well as us. / … as we did.
5   I’ve been here longer than you.
You    .
You haven’t been here as long as me. / … as I have.
6   She’s more nervous than him.
He    .
He isn’t as nervous as her. / … as she is.
Unit 89   Exercise 3
Write as or than.
1   Athens is older  than  Rome.
2   I don’t watch TV as much    you.
I don’t watch TV as much as you.
3   You eat more    me.
You eat more than me.
4   I’m more tired today    I was yesterday.
I’m more tired today than I was yesterday.
5   Joe isn’t as intelligent    he thinks.
Joe isn’t as intelligent as he thinks.
6   Belgium is smaller    Switzerland.
Belgium is smaller than Switzerland.
7   Brazil isn’t as big    Canada.
Brazil isn’t as big as Canada.
8   I can’t wait longer    an hour.
I can’t wait longer than an hour.
