Unit 91   Exercise 4
Complete the sentences.  Use enough with these words:
1 We haven’t got  enough money to buy  a new car. (money/buy)
2 This knife isn’t    tomatoes. (sharp/cut)

3 The water wasn’t    swimming. (warm/go)
The water wasn’t warm enough to go swimming.
4 Have we got    sandwiches? (bread/make)
Have we got enough bread to make sandwiches?
5 We played well, but not    the game. (well/win)
We played well, but not well enough to win the game.
6 I don’t have    newspapers. (time/read)
I don’t have enough time to read newspapers.
