1   I stayed at home and watched TV.
2   I bought a sandwich, but I didn’t eat it.
3   I  
I went to the window and (I) looked out.
4   
I wanted to phone you, but I didn’t have your number.
5   
I jumped into the river and (I) swam to the other side.
6   
I usually drive to work, but I went by bus this morning.
7   
Do you want me to come with you, or shall I wait here?

Example answers
1   It was very hot,  so he opened the window.
2   They couldn’t play tennis  
They couldn’t play tennis because it was raining. / … because the
weather was bad.
3   They went to the museum,  
They went to the museum, but it was closed.
4   Ben wasn’t hungry,  
Ben wasn’t hungry, so he didn’t eat anything. / … so he didn’t want
anything to eat.
5   Helen was late  
Helen was late because there was a lot of traffic. / … because the
traffic was bad.
6   Sue said  
Sue said goodbye, got into her car and drove  off / away.
Unit 97   Exercise 3
Write sentences about what you did yesterday.  Use and/but etc.
Example answers
1 (and) In the evening I stayed at home and studied.
2 (because) I went to bed very early because I was tired.
3 (but)
I went to the cinema, but the film wasn’t very good.
4 (and)
I went to a café and met some friends of mine.
5 (so)
There was a film on television, so I watched it.
6 (because)
I got up in the middle of the night because I couldn’t sleep.
