Unit 99   Exercise 4
Write if or when.
1   If  I’m late this evening, don’t wait for me.
2   I’m going to do some shopping now.    I come back, we can have lunch.
I’m going to do some shopping now.  When I come back, we can have lunch.
3   I’m thinking of going to see Tom.    I go, will you come with me?
I’m thinking of going to see Tom.  If I go, will you come with me?
4     you don’t want to go out tonight, we can stay at home.
If you don’t want to go out tonight, we can stay at home.
5   Is it OK    I close the window?
Is it OK if I close the window?
6   John is still at school.    he leaves school, he wants to go to college.
John is still at school.  When he leaves school, he wants to go to college.
7   Shall we have a picnic tomorrow    the weather is good?
Shall we have a picnic tomorrow if the weather is good?
8   We’re going to Madrid next week.  We don’t have anywhere to stay – we hope to
find a hotel    we get there.  I don’t know what we’ll do    we
don’t find a room.
We’re going to Madrid next week.  We don’t have anywhere to stay – we hope to find a hotel when we get there.  I don’t know what we’ll do if we don’t find a room.
