Unit 103   Exercise 2
Write at/on/in.
1   Bye! See you  on  Friday.
2   Where were you    28 February?
Where were you on 28 February?
3   I got up    8 o’clock this morning.
I got up at 8 o’clock this morning.
4   I like getting up early    the morning.
I like getting up early in the morning.
5   My sister got married    May.
My sister got married in May.
6   Jessica and I first met    2006.
Jessica and I first met in 2006.
7   Did you go out    Tuesday?
Did you go out on Tuesday?
8   Did you go out    Tuesday evening?
Did you go out on Tuesday evening?
9   Do you often go out    the evening?
Do you often go out in the evening?
10   Let’s meet    7.30 tomorrow evening.
Let’s meet at 7.30 tomorrow evening.
11   I often go away    the weekend.
I often go away at* the weekend.
12   I’m starting my new job    3 July.
I’m starting my new job on 3 July.
13   We often go to the beach    summer.
We often go to the beach in summer.
14   George isn’t here    the moment.
George isn’t here at the moment.
15   Jane’s birthday is    December.
Jane’s birthday is in December.
16   Do you work    Saturdays?
Do you work on Saturdays?
17   The company started    1999.
The company started in 1999.
18   I like to look at the stars    night.
I like to look at the stars at night.
19   I’ll send you the money    the end of the month.
I’ll send you the money at the end of the month.
* American speakers say ‘on the weekend’.
