the course the night
1   Everybody was nervous  before the exam  .
2   I usually work four hours in the morning, and another three hours    .
I usually work four hours in the morning, and another three hours after
lunch.
3   The film was really boring.  We left    .
The film was really boring.  We left before the end.

Anna went to evening classes to learn German.  She learnt a lot during the
course.

Unit 105   Exercise 3
Complete the sentences.  Use -ing (doing, having etc.).
1   After  doing  the shopping, they went home.
2   I felt sick after    too much chocolate.
I felt sick after eating too much chocolate.
3   I’m going to ask you a question.  Think carefully before    it.
I’m going to ask you a question.  Think carefully before answering it.
4   I felt awful when I got up this morning.  I felt better after    a shower.
I felt awful when I got up this morning.  I felt better after  having / taking  a
shower.
5   After    my work, I left the office and went home.
After  finishing / doing  my work, I left the office and went home.
6   Before    to a foreign country, it’s good to try and learn a little of the
language.
Before  going / travelling  to a foreign country, it’s good to try and learn a
little of the language.
