BECKY

FRANK
1   Carl is standing  behind  Frank.
2   Frank is sitting    Emma.
Frank is sitting  next to / beside / by  Emma.
3   Emma is sitting    Becky.
Emma is sitting in front of Becky.
4   Emma is sitting    Daniela and Frank.
Emma is sitting between Daniela and Frank.
5   Daniela is sitting    Emma.
Daniela is sitting  next to / beside / by  Emma.
6   Frank is sitting    Carl.
Frank is sitting in front of Carl.
7   Alan is standing    Daniela.
Alan is standing behind Daniela.
8   Alan is standing    left.
Alan is standing on the left.
9   Becky is standing    middle.
Becky is standing in the middle.
Unit 109   Exercise 2
Look at the pictures and complete the sentences.
1   The cat is  under  the table.
2   There is a big tree    the house.
There is a big tree behind the house.
3   The plane is flying    the clouds.

4   She is standing    the piano.
She is standing in front of the piano.
5   The cinema is    the right.
The cinema is on the right.
6   She’s standing    the fridge.

7   The switch is    the window.
The switch is  below / under  the window.
8   The cupboard is    the sink.
The cupboard is above the sink.
9   There are some shoes    the bed.
There are some shoes under the bed.
10   The plant is    the piano.
The plant is  by / next to / beside  the piano.
11   Paul is sitting    Fiona.
Paul is sitting opposite Fiona.
12   In Britain people drive    the left.
In Britain people drive on the left.
