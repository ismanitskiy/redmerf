Unit 110   Exercise 2
Look at the pictures and complete the sentences.
1   The dog swam  across  the river.
 
2   A book fell    the shelf.
A book fell off the shelf.
3   A plane flew    the village.
A plane flew over the village.
4   A woman got    the car.
A woman got out of the car.
5   A girl ran    the road.
A girl ran across the road.
6   Suddenly a car came    the corner.
Suddenly a car came  round / around  the corner.
7   They drove    the village.
They drove through the village.
8   They got    the train.
They got on the train.
9   The moon travels    the earth.
The moon travels  round / around  the earth.
10   They got    the house    a window.
They got into the house through a window.
Unit 110   Exercise 3
Complete the sentences.  Use over/from/into etc.
1   I looked    the window and watched the people in the street.
I looked out of the window and watched the people in the street.
2   My house is very near here.  It’s just    the corner.
My house is very near here. It’s just  round / around  the corner.
3   ‘Where’s my phone?’     ‘You put it    your bag.’
‘Where’s my phone?’    ‘You put it in your bag.’
4   How far is it    here    the airport?
How far is it from here to the airport?
5   We walked    the museum for an hour and saw a lot of interesting things.
We walked  round / around  the museum for an hour and saw a lot of
interesting things.
6   You can put your coat    the back of the chair.
You can put your coat  on / over  the back of the chair.
7   In tennis, you have to hit the ball    the net.
In tennis, you have to hit the ball over the net.
8   Silvia took a key    her bag and opened the door.
Silvia took a key  out of / from  her bag and opened the door.
